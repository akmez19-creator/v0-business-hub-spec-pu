# **Business Hub - Complete Application Specification**

## **1. PROJECT OVERVIEW**

**Name:** Business Hub - Enterprise Management Platform**Purpose:** Centralized platform for managing marketing, procurement, inventory, sales, logistics, and delivery operations for a Mauritius-based business.**Currency:** MUR (Mauritian Rupee) - displayed as "Rs X,XXX"**Phone Format:** +230 XXXX XXXX**Language:** English

---

## **2. USER ROLES & PERMISSIONS**

### **Role Hierarchy (highest to lowest)**

| Role | Level | Description
|-----|-----|-----
| `admin` | 4 | Full system access, user management, all modules
| `manager` | 3 | All operational modules, can view all data
| `marketing_agent` | 2 | Marketing, sales, products modules
| `contractor` | 2 | Logistics - manages riders, assigns deliveries
| `rider` | 1 | Logistics - sees only their assigned deliveries


### **Module Access by Role**

```plaintext
admin: facebook-ads, product-master, settings, orders, admin, logistics, sales, clients
manager: facebook-ads, product-master, orders, logistics, sales, clients
marketing_agent: facebook-ads, product-master, sales, clients
contractor: logistics, orders (own riders only)
rider: logistics (own deliveries only)
```

### **Login Behavior**

- **Admin/Manager/Marketing Agent:** See full dashboard at `/` with module cards
- **Contractor:** Auto-redirect to `/logistics/contractor-dashboard` (mobile-first UI)
- **Rider:** Auto-redirect to `/logistics/rider-dashboard` (mobile-first UI)
